Research

Compositionally Complex Battery Materials

Designing and synthesizing complex oxide materials for sodium-ion and lithium-ion batteries, with emphasis on cathode chemistry, phase stability, and electrochemical performance.

Layered Sodium Cathodes

Studying P2-type and O3-type sodium layered cathodes, including structural evolution, voltage behavior, transition-metal ordering, and phase transitions during cycling.

Lab Automation and High-Throughput Synthesis

Building automated and semi-automated workflows for synthesis planning, precursor calculation, data organization, and materials characterization.

AI-Driven Materials Discovery

Applying data-driven methods and machine learning tools to accelerate composition screening, structure-property analysis, and experimental decision-making.
